Themes

Four themes. One feeling.

Switch anytime. The whole app responds instantly.

Stark Dark

The default.

Near-black canvas, hairline white borders, monochromatic accent. Designed to disappear and let your content lead.

Stark Light

Same restraint, inverted.

White cards on a near-white canvas. Deep slate text. For those who work in daylight.

Calm Earth

Softer. Warmer.

Cream canvas, olive accents, and a serif body that breathes. For when the day calls for something quieter.

Liquid Glass

The showpiece.

Frosted glass cards over a lavender-to-teal gradient. A specular edge that catches light. This one's for the lock screen.

Switcher

Try it.

Tap a theme. The mockup updates. So does this page.
