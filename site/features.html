Features

Five tabs. One vocabulary.

Tasks, notes, goals, calendar, widgets. Every surface speaks the same language as the capture bar.

Tasks

Your to-do list, without the ceremony.

Swipe to complete. Swipe to delete. Filter by type. Link tasks to goals. No folders, no projects, no setup.

Notes

Brain dumps that stay put.

Markdown rendered on the fly. Write in raw syntax, read in clean prose. Tap to edit, tap again to read.

Goals

Measure what matters.

Set a target. Pick a unit. Swipe to increment. Watch the progress rail move. Link tasks to goals so everything connects.

Calendar

See your week without the dread.

A month grid with colored dots for each task type. Tap a day, see your tasks. No subscriptions, no sync headaches.

Widgets

Your day on your home screen.

Small, medium, and large widgets. Updates every 15 minutes. Tap to open the capture bar directly from your home screen.

One inbox. Four lenses.

Avenor doesn't make you choose. It just makes you type.
